What is BevBuilder?

We are a web based education company built by bartenders for bartender. Wether you are just getting behind the bar for the first time, or want to brush up on your skills, we have something for everyone, at every skill level. Start your journey with the bare basics. What spirits are. Where they came from. Then hone your bar techniques as you work through our video classrooms. For each segment there are videos, recipes, and history of the cocktails and spirits. There is also included a skill check test at the end of each segment to make sure that the knowledge you picked up is retained.
