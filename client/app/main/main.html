In A Nutshell

BevBuilder is a beverage education platform built for bartenders by bartenders. With inspiring content designed to help you understand the why behind the what, BevBuilder will help guide you along your journey to becoming a more confident bartender.

Basics

Whether you're just getting started or are a seasoned pro, mastering the basics is essential. This course is here to help you learn the fundamentals of bartending, BevBuilder style.

Classics

Shaking, stirring, twists and flips. We'll teach you the correct techniques of the trade and when to use them right here.

Modern Mixology

What is whiskey? When do you shake a drink, and what is a twist? This is your resource for spirits, technique, history, glassware and more.
